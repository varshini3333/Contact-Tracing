Contact Tracing is a crucial tool in combating infectious diseases
such as COVID-19,Tuberculosis by identifying and notifying individuals
exposed to infected persons to prevent further transmission.Public
health strives to reduce illnesses in the population by tracing the

helps break the chain of infection and slow the diseases spread.
